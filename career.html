Position : HR Recruiter / Sr. Recruiter

Qualification

  • B.E./M.B.A/M.C.A/C.A-Preferred.
  • Graduation/Post Graduation in any discipline
  • Freshers are Welcome

Job description

  • Responsible for entire recruitment cycle right from searching / sourcing and screening resumes to short-listing candidates, coordinating, & conducting interviews
  • Identification of the best sourcing methodologies according to the position –Job portals, Candidate reference, Database, Networking, Linkedin, Head Hunting, etc.
  • Conducting and coordinating recruitment drives
  • Negotiating on the offer amount
  • Maintaining CV database
  • Preparing daily tracker reports

Position : Key Account Manager- HR/Sr. Key Account Manager-HR

Qualification

  • B.E./M.B.A/M.C.A/C.A-Preferred.
  • Graduation/Post Graduation in any discipline
  • Freshers are Welcome

Job description

Client Management

  • Regularly interacting with the clients to understand their requirements and source relevant profiles accordingly
  • Taking feedback and inputs from the client

Recruitment

  • Responsible for entire recruitment cycle process right from receiving the client requirements, understanding, strategizing the search process on the job sites, scanning the CVs, arranging interviews, soliciting timely feedback & maintaining Client/Candidature information
  • Identification of the best sourcing methodologies according to the position –Job portals, Candidate reference, Database, Networking, Head Hunting, Cold calling etc.
  • Conducting and coordinating recruitment drives in different cities
  • Co-ordination between the client and the candidate
  • Negotiating on the offer amount with the candidates and client.

Team management

  • Supervising and mentoring team of recruiters
